About

Product leadership shaped by engineering, AI, and business strategy.

MBA candidate at UW Foster with 6+ years of experience across product management and software engineering in financial services, healthcare AI, and enterprise platforms. I bring a rare mix of engineering depth, product ownership, and business strategy — from launching trading workflow platforms at Citi to building AI-driven clinical intake systems and leading high-stakes product work across technical and strategic environments.

I started in engineering at Citibank, building internal platforms that handled large-scale transaction flows and release pipelines. That foundation still shapes how I work today: I like ambitious ideas, but I care just as much about technical clarity, execution risk, and measurable outcomes.

Since moving into product, I've led platform, AI, and workflow initiatives across FinTech and healthcare while sharpening the strategy side through my MBA at UW Foster. The through-line is simple: find operational pain, align the right teams, and turn complexity into shipped products people actually use.
